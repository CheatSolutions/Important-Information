- Compatible: Broadly supports popular plugins (mcMMO, Essentials, Geyser/Floodgate, ItemsAdder, etc.). Note: Some are PARTIAL or THIRD-PARTY.

[COMMANDS & PERMISSIONS]
- UX: Supports TAB auto-complete and in-game hover descriptions.
- Admin/Staff Perms (`spartan.admin`, `spartan.*`): Grants all perms EXCEPT bypasses. Be careful when OP.
- Bypass Perms: `spartan.bypass` (all), `spartan.bypass.[check]` (specific), `spartan.punishment` (bypass punishments only), `spartan.bedrock` (force Bedrock client ID). Use `op_bypass` in settings.yml for OPs.
- Core Commands (`/spartan ...`):
  - `panic`: Silent mode, disables all punishments.
  - `syn`: Enable Syn GUIs.
  - `reload`: Reload config.
  - `notifications [ticks]` & `verbose`: Toggle alert frequency/verbosity.
  - `info [player]`: Open violations GUI.
  - `kick` / `warn`: Kick or warn players.
  - `toggle`, `toggle-prevention`, `toggle-punishment`: Granularly toggle checks or their outcomes.
  - `bypass <player> <check> <seconds>`: Temporary bypass.
  - `wave <add/remove/clear/run/list>`: Manage punishment waves.
  - `proxy-command`: Send command to proxy. (Requires Spigot proxy plugins).
  - Conditionals: `<player> if <cond> <operator> <result> do <command>`.
